Laid-back Altina

Laid-back Altina is a sticker set orginally released in Japanese on LINE by Nihon Falcom, featuring Altina Orion from the Trails of Cold Steel series. Having been a fan of the set, I took the liberty of translating them into English for others to use. I also went a step further and added some additional stickers to the set to sweeten things up.

Now you can enjoy Laid-back Altina in English with 25% MORE PANCAKES!

Click the button below to download the Laid-back Altina sticker set. The contents of the download are listed below the button along with a preview.

Contents

  • 41 Clean Stickers (no texts)
  • 42 English Stickers + 10 Bonus Stickers
  • 40 Japanese Stickers (original) + 10 Bonus Stickers not in the LINE set
  • Twitter folder with all stickers above having their canvas resized to 600x320 to avoid the terrible image stretching on Twitter and similar websites.
  • Misc files: README, Translation Notes, Previews for English and Japanese sets

Total Size: about 5mb or 5 servings of pancakes
