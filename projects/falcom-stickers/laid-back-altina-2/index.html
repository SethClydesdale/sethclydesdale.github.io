Laid-back Altina 2

Laid-back Altina 2 is a sticker set originally released in Japanese on LINE by Nihon Falcom, featuring Altina Orion from the Trails of Cold Steel series. Being a big fan of Altina, I once again took the liberty of translating them into English for others to use!
